Additional Details:

Purpose: Establish luxurious, medium-haul routes utilizing stopovers and maximizing the aircraft's range.

Main configurations for most of the aircrafts consist of high First Class and Business Class seating rather than Economy Class seating.

Medium-haul consists of locations to small and large airports within the aicraft's capabilities.

Advantages: Quick flow of profits in a short amount of time. Great for medium-haul routes with high demand, which turns into great profitability. Higher ticket prices for First and Business Classes.

Disadvantages: High fuel consumption (14 lbs/km), low passenger seating per flight (90 pax - single class configuration), and low range (2,036 km). Great to start for establishing a new airline. However, the aircraft becomes more of a hinderance rather than an asset once establishing a bigger fleet. High maintenance (340 flight hours before A-Check).

Roughly ~$900,000 per aircraft * 30 total DC-9-10s = ~$27,000,000 spent on the DC-9-10 fleet.

Return of Investment (ROI) Status: Positive
